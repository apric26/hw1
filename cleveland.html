Cleveland, OH

O-Me-O, O-My-O, Oh Cleveland, Ohio! This quote from The West Wing's CJ Craig was my only reference to Cleveland. With only a few days before I had to head back to Chicago for Christmas, I made the most of my visit. I played shuffleboard at a local Cleveland spot after the bowling alley was closed for the afternoon. I also enjoyed a few local brews while there! Looking forward to heading back when the weather is nicer!
