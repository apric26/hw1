Santiago, Chile

After a hectic arrival, I landed in Santiago, Chile on January 2, 2018 for a Kellogg Exchange program. Only in country for one week, I took advantage of the free time we had outside of class time. I visited a look-out point near school, which had great views of Santiago. I also went to an internationally acclaimed restaurant, Boca Nariz, for a evening of great Chilean cuisine and wine pairings. What I appreciated the most from this exchange experience was getting to know the other Kellogg students better. I would recommend Kellogg Exchange to everyone!!
